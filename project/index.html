About Me

I'm a highly motivated and enthusiastic Full Stack Web Developer with a strong foundation in front-end technologies and a growing interest in full-stack development. My journey began with a curiosity for how websites work, and that curiosity quickly evolved into a passion for building interactive and visually appealing digital experiences.

I graduated from Bapatla Women's Engineering College, after completing my intermediate studies at Sri Chaitanya Junior College and schooling at Bhashyam High School. Throughout my education, I focused on learning the core principles of web development and worked on practical projects that helped me solidify my understanding of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and essential development tools like GitHub, Figma, and Visual Studio Code.

I enjoy solving real-world problems through code and continuously challenge myself to grow both technically and creatively. Whether it's designing a clean UI or debugging a tricky script, I love the process of building things that people find useful and enjoyable. In the future, I aim to expand my expertise into backend technologies and explore full-stack development even further.
